About Us

While travel agencies traditionally focused on booking flights and accommodations, many now offer online train ticket sales for domestic and international travel. This provides a convenient one-stop shop for planning your entire journey. Here's how it works: Through a travel agency's website or app, you can search for train routes between your desired origin and destination. They often aggregate fares from various train operators, allowing you to compare prices and travel times efficiently. Some agencies may even offer exclusive deals or discounts on train tickets. Once you've chosen your preferred train and travel class, you can proceed with a secure online payment using your credit card or debit card. The agency will handle the booking process with the train operator and provide you with e-tickets or confirmation emails for your trip. Travel agencies can also be helpful if you're planning a complex train journey with multiple legs or connections. They can assist you in piecing together the itinerary and ensuring smooth transfers between trains. However, it's important to compare prices offered by the agency with those on the train operator's website directly, as the agency might charge additional booking fees.
